What is Prolog?!

Declarative vs. procedural programming
High-level programming
Based on formal logic--defining then solving logical formulas.
Facts (end in .) -- Rules (end in :- ) -- Queries (user input, begins with ? -)





About Prolog

Phillippe Roussel
1971
Marseilles, France!
Designed to implement a man-machine communication system in natural language. Short for Programmation en Logique












Uses and Application

Used for Artificial Intelligence, especially expert systems.

"Expert Systems" are computer systems that can mimic the actions of a human expert.
i.e. a program that can diagnose a patient similar to a doctor.




Example!

loves(ron,hermione).
loves(harry,hermione).
loves(voldemort,nagini).
loves(hagrid,madameMaxime).
jealous(X,Y):- loves(X,Z), loves(Y,Z)
//:- refers to if/implied by
?- loves(voldemort,nagini)
Yes
?- loves(ron,nagini)
No
?- jealous(ron,W)
//Can you find who Ron would be jealous of?
W = harry
